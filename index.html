Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 8 oz
Bacon 6 slices
Eggs 2
Grated Parmesan Cheese 1/2 cup
Black Pepper To taste

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions; drain.
  2. Cook bacon in a skillet until crisp; remove and crumble.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with bacon grease.
  5. Remove skillet from heat and stir in egg mixture until coated.
